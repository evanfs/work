EVAN STRATER

As a multi-disciplinary designer, I have a background in brand, web, and traditional graphic design. Inspired by experiences that we can touch and interact with, I made the transition to product design in 2014.

Over these past 7 years, I’ve been fortunate to work with the incredibly talented product design, research, and strategy team at Studio Science. I’ve worked on diverse end-to-end design initatives, across B2B and consumer: from research and analaysis, to synthesis and solution prototyping, and driving usabilty with user testing. Project work includes industry leaders like Cummins, Angie’s List, Genesys, and Salesforce.

Outside of the office, I recharge with my wife and two children (3 and 9). I enjoy working on projects around the home and getting outdoors to unplug.
